YES! WE ARE THE
SCHOOL OF DESIGN

Located at the heart of Downtown Toronto, the School of Design at George Brown College is a place where dreams comes to life.

The School of Design resides within a 103,000-square-foot facility in the Daniels Waterfront – City of the Arts development that houses our School of Design programs and a research hub. We value inclusivity, collaboration, and the power of good design to make an impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

What programs does the School of Design offer?

The School of Design offers various programs including Graphic Design, UX/UI Design, Game Design, and Motion Graphics. You can check out all the programs we are offering by clicking to this link.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes! We offer a variety of scholarships based on academic performance, financial need, and design competitions.

What are the admission requirements for these programs?

Admission requirements vary by program. Generally, applicants need an Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD) or equivalent, including specific course prerequisites. Some programs may also require a portfolio or additional assessments.

How do I apply to the School of Design?

Applications are submitted through the Ontario Colleges Application Service (ontariocolleges.ca). Ensure you meet all program-specific requirements and submit any necessary documents, such as transcripts or portfolios.

or you can click here to go to the application page.

What support services are available to students?

George Brown College offers various support services, including academic advising, counseling, career services, and technical support, to assist students throughout their studies.
