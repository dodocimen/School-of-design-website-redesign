Industry Experience at School of Design

At George Brown College’s School of Design, students don’t just learn in the classroom, they gain real experience working with clients and industry professionals. Our Digital Media and Gaming Incubator and StudioLab programs help students build skills, connect with employers, and prepare for careers in design.

Students dive into real-world experiences through hands-on projects, industry placements, paid research opportunities, networking events, guest lectures, and conferences, gaining the skills and connections that shape their future.

What is the Digital Media and Gaming Incubator?

The Digital Media and Gaming Incubator is a hub that supports Toronto’s digital sector by offering startups affordable space, business services, and access to technology. It provides a collaborative environment where entrepreneurs can work, network, and grow their ideas.

Where is the Incubator located?

The Incubator is located at 3 Lower Jarvis (second floor), along Toronto’s waterfront. It shares space with the School of Design’s programs, fostering a creative and innovative atmosphere.

What makes the Incubator different from a regular coworking space?

Beyond just providing office space, the Incubator is designed to encourage both focused work and collaboration. It offers a dynamic environment where startups can engage in formal and informal exchanges, explore new ideas, and build meaningful connections with like-minded professionals.

How can I apply for office space or a hotdesk?

If you’re interested in securing office space or a hotdesk at the Incubator, please reach out to incubator@georgebrown.ca for more details and availability.
